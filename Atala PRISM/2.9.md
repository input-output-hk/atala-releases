## Changelog

**Updated components:**

- Open Enterprise Agent: v1.21.0 -> v1.25.0
- Documentation Portal: v1.69.0 -> v1.76.0
- Apollo Crypto Library: v1.1
- Wallet SDK KMM: no changes
